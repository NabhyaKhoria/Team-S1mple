About Hall

Pandit Madan Mohan Malaviya Hall Of Residence is the second largest Hall Of Residence in IIT Kharagpur. It was founded on 26th February, 2005. There are four blocks - SDS, RR, DSK and HK blocks. It resides students across diverse fields that include UG, PG, MBA and Law.
